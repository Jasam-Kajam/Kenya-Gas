Emergency Help

Suspected Gas Leak?

If you smell gas or suspect a leak:

  • Do NOT light matches or use lighters.
  • Do NOT switch electrical appliances on or off.
  • Open doors and windows immediately.
  • Turn off the gas cylinder valve if it is safe to do so.
  • Move everyone away from the affected area.
  • Call emergency services or your gas supplier immediately.
